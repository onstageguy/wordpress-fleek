What does Oppai, nsfw, waifu, weeb, otaku, mean?

OPPAI – Oppai is a term originating from Japanese culture that refers to breasts or busty characters in anime, manga, and related art forms.

NSFW – Stands for “Not Safe For Work” and is used to label content that may contain explicit or inappropriate material unsuitable for viewing in professional or public environments.

WAIFU – In anime and gaming culture, “waifu” refers to a fictional female character, often from anime or video games, whom someone has a strong emotional attachment to or considers as their ideal romantic partner.

WEEB – The term “weeb” is a colloquial abbreviation for “weeaboo,” which refers to a non-Japanese individual who has a strong fascination or obsession with Japanese culture, particularly anime, manga, and other related media.

OTAKU – It’s a Japanese term used to describe individuals with an intense passion or obsession for a particular interest, often related to anime, manga, video games, or other aspects of Japanese popular culture. In Western contexts, it is commonly used to refer to avid fans of anime and manga.
