OPPAI<\/strong> – Oppai is a term originating from Japanese culture that refers to breasts or busty characters in anime, manga, and related art forms.<\/p>

NSFW<\/strong> – Stands for “Not Safe For Work” and is used to label content that may contain explicit or inappropriate material unsuitable for viewing in professional or public environments.<\/p>

WAIFU<\/strong> – In anime and gaming culture, “waifu” refers to a fictional female character, often from anime or video games, whom someone has a strong emotional attachment to or considers as their ideal romantic partner.<\/p>

WEEB<\/strong> – The term “weeb” is a colloquial abbreviation for “weeaboo,” which refers to a non-Japanese individual who has a strong fascination or obsession with Japanese culture, particularly anime, manga, and other related media.<\/p>

OTAKU<\/strong> – It’s a Japanese term used to describe individuals with an intense passion or obsession for a particular interest, often related to anime, manga, video games, or other aspects of Japanese popular culture.
